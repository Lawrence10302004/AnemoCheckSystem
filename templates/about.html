About AnemoCheck

AnemoCheck is an AI-powered diagnostic system designed to help detect and assess anemia through analysis of Complete Blood Count (CBC) parameters. Using machine learning models, it evaluates hemoglobin, red and white blood cells, hematocrit, MCV, MCH, MCHC, and platelet count based on World Health Organization (WHO) standards to deliver accurate and data-driven results.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make anemia detection more accessible and user-friendly. We aim to empower individuals to monitor their blood health through clear, insightful analytics â€” encouraging early action and better health awareness.

How It Works

AnemoCheck processes CBC test data through a machine learning algorithm trained to identify signs of anemia. By combining hemoglobin and other blood indices, it delivers both a diagnostic result and actionable recommendations for users.

Users can also monitor their past test results through historical data tracking and visualization, enabling them to stay informed about their overall blood health trends.
